Main Content

The Eiffel Tower is the most beautiful sight in Paris. It is simply breathtaking and at night it looks gorgeous as it lights up the place. You can go anywhere you want in Paris, but this place is a must see.
The Louvre Museum is as breathtaking as the Eiffel Tower. It is a historic art museum that has tons of amazing art and is one of the best sights to see in Paris.
The Paris Zoo is another amazing place to vist in Paris. This a great place for the family and this is a fun place for kids to meet their favorite animals.
